GITHUB UPLOAD — BOOK BUTTON FIX

1. Upload book-cover-fix.css into the main/root area of your repository.
   It should sit beside index.html, style.css, and script.js.

2. Open index.html and locate:
   <link rel="stylesheet" href="style.css">

3. Directly under it, add:
   <link rel="stylesheet" href="book-cover-fix.css">

4. Commit the changes.

This fix:
- Keeps the View Back Cover button below the book.
- Shrinks and spaces the back-cover copy so it stays inside the book.
- Prevents the text and button from overlapping.
- Improves the mobile layout.
